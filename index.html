ABOUT

Data Infra for ML Systems

I’m a Data Engineer with experience building production pipelines using Azure Data Factory, Databricks, and Apache NiFi—plus hands-on ML work that taught me a simple truth: most model failures are data failures. I’m currently pursuing an M.S. in Data Science at the University of Maryland, where I’m focused on time-series ML, deep learning, and end-to-end systems.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering discipline and model-aware thinking: incremental ingestion, schema evolution, medallion/lakehouse design, quality checks, and observability—so data stays trustworthy as systems scale.

I’m especially interested in building platforms that are secure by default (RBAC, audit logs, governed layers) and intelligent by design (drift checks, anomaly detection, and automation that reduces operational burden).
